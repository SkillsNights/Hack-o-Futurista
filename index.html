Hack-o-Futurista


Hackathon curated by Skills Nights, with an expected participation of 1000+.Hack-o-Futurista is an unprecedented 2-stage hackathon spanning over a week where the teams need to first send us their ideas and the best ones picked by our judges will move on to the next stage to implement the same. This will be the final hurdle for the participants and then we'll be announcing the ingenios winners of the hackathon from the list curated by our judges.

Skills Nights


Skills Nights is an ambitious student club that primarly aims at providing a beginner-friendly platform,making it an ideal starting point for the freshers to join and begin their journey of learning/exploring new technologies in their field of interest, building projects,and collaborating with their fellow batchmates to make awesome projects that help them stand out.

TantraFiesta


The TantraFiesta is the National Level Technical Fest of the IIIT Nagpur. The most awaited event of IIIT Nagpur. This National Level Technical Fest comprises different technical events and keynotes by the industry experts.
